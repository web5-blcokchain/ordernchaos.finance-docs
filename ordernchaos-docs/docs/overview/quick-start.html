Quick Start

Get started with OrderNChaos in minutes

Previous: What's the Difference Next: Genesis Launch Auction(GLA)

Last updated 3 years ago

ONC produces the $CHAOS token, which is an investment instrument designed for sustainable gains and high APY staking.

The most straightforward way to earn with ONC is:

  • Purchase $CHAOS through the protocol's instant-buy market.
  • Stake the $CHAOS and begin earning $prCHAOS rewards
  • Claim your $prCHAOS rewards and realize them into $CHAOS
  • Stake the newly acquired $CHAOS for compounding interest on your $prCHAOS rewards
  • Mint a $ORDER loan from your staked $CHAOS, and use that $ORDER to buy more $CHAOS, levering up your exposure with zero liquidation risk.

Getting Started Steps

1. Connect your wallet

Visit the OrderNChaos app and connect your wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, or other supported wallets).

2. Purchase $CHAOS

Navigate to the "Buy" section and purchase $CHAOS tokens using your preferred stablecoin (USDC, USDT, DAI, etc.).

3. Stake your $CHAOS

Go to the "Stake" section and stake your $CHAOS tokens to start earning $prCHAOS rewards.

4. Claim and compound

Periodically claim your $prCHAOS rewards and convert them to $CHAOS. Stake the newly acquired $CHAOS to compound your returns.

5. Leverage with $ORDER (optional)

For advanced users: mint $ORDER loans against your staked $CHAOS, and use the $ORDER to purchase more $CHAOS, increasing your staking position and rewards.

These simple steps allow you to benefit from the sustainable APY and rising floor price of the OrderNChaos protocol.
