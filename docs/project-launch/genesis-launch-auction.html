Introduction to the Genesis Launch Auction

The Genesis Launch Auction (GLA) is the fair and transparent mechanism through which the OrderNChaos protocol will distribute its initial supply of $CHAOS tokens. Unlike traditional token launches that often favor insiders or large investors, the GLA is designed to give everyone an equal opportunity to participate while establishing a market-driven initial price for $CHAOS.

Key Benefits of the GLA

  • Fair Distribution: All participants have equal access, with no preferential treatment
  • Price Discovery: The market determines the initial price of $CHAOS
  • Protocol Bootstrapping: Establishes the initial reserve that backs the floor price of $CHAOS
  • Community Building: Fosters a community of aligned stakeholders from day one

How the Genesis Launch Auction Works

Auction Mechanics

The GLA follows a modified batch auction format:

  1. Duration: The auction runs for 7 days (168 hours) to ensure global participation
  2. Participation: Participants contribute stablecoins (USDC, USDT, DAI) to the auction pool
  3. Dynamic Pricing: The final price is determined by total contributions divided by the number of $CHAOS tokens allocated for the auction
  4. Token Distribution: At the end of the auction, participants receive $CHAOS tokens proportional to their contribution
  5. Reserve Establishment: All contributed stablecoins become the initial reserve backing the floor price of $CHAOS

Auction Formula

The number of $CHAOS tokens a participant receives is calculated using the formula:

CHAOS_received = User_contribution / Final_price

Where:

  • CHAOS_received: The amount of $CHAOS tokens the participant receives
  • User_contribution: The amount of stablecoins the participant contributed
  • Final_price: The clearing price determined at the end of the auction (Total contributions / Total $CHAOS allocated)

GLA Allocation and Parameters

Token Allocation

The GLA will distribute 10% of the total $CHAOS supply (10,000,000 $CHAOS tokens).

Example Scenario

If the total contributions to the GLA amount to $20,000,000, the clearing price would be:

Final_price = $20,000,000 / 10,000,000 $CHAOS = $2 per $CHAOS

A participant who contributed $10,000 would receive:

CHAOS_received = $10,000 / $2 = 5,000 $CHAOS

Minimum and Maximum Contribution

  • Minimum Contribution: $100 equivalent in stablecoins
  • Maximum Contribution: None (to allow for institutional participation)

Accepted Stablecoins

  • USDC
  • USDT
  • DAI

How to Participate in the GLA

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Preparation: Ensure you have stablecoins (USDC, USDT, or DAI) in your wallet and enough ETH for gas fees
  2. Visit the Auction Site: Go to https://gla.ordernchaos.finance during the auction period
  3. Connect Wallet: Connect your Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
  4. Specify Contribution: Enter the amount of stablecoins you wish to contribute
  5. Approve Tokens: Approve the auction contract to use your stablecoins
  6. Confirm Contribution: Confirm your contribution transaction
  7. Track Progress: Monitor the auction dashboard for real-time statistics
  8. Receive $CHAOS: After the auction ends, claim your $CHAOS tokens through the same interface

Auction Dashboard Features

  • Total Contributions: Real-time tracking of all stablecoins contributed
  • Current Price: The estimated clearing price based on current contributions
  • Time Remaining: Countdown to the end of the auction
  • Your Contribution: Summary of your personal contributions
  • Estimated Allocation: Projected amount of $CHAOS you will receive

Post-Auction Protocol Launch

After the successful completion of the GLA, the OrderNChaos protocol will officially launch with the following features available immediately:

  • $CHAOS Trading: Buy and sell $CHAOS through the protocol's vAMM
  • Staking: Stake $CHAOS to earn $prCHAOS rewards
  • Zero Risk Loans: Mint $ORDER stablecoins against staked $CHAOS
  • Analytics Dashboard: View key protocol metrics and your personal statistics

The initial floor price of $CHAOS will be set based on the GLA results, providing immediate stability and downside protection for all token holders.

FAQs about the Genesis Launch Auction

What happens if I contribute multiple times?

All your contributions will be added together for the purpose of calculating your $CHAOS allocation. You can contribute as many times as you wish during the auction period.

Can I withdraw my contribution during the auction?

No, all contributions are final and cannot be withdrawn once confirmed. This ensures fairness and prevents last-minute manipulation of the auction.

What if the auction doesn't reach a minimum threshold?

There is no minimum threshold for the GLA. The auction will proceed regardless of the total amount contributed, and the clearing price will be calculated accordingly.

Will there be any bonuses for early participants?

No, all participants will receive the same price regardless of when they contribute during the auction period. This ensures fairness for participants across all time zones.

How is the floor price of $CHAOS determined after the auction?

The initial floor price will be set at a percentage of the clearing price, with the exact ratio determined by the protocol parameters. This creates immediate stability and downside protection.
