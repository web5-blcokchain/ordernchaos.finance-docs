Staking Rewards ($prCHAOS)

Understanding the staking mechanism and $prCHAOS rewards

Previous: Pricing Curve(vAMM) Next: Zero Risk Loans ($ORDER)

Last updated 3 years ago

Introduction to Staking in OrderNChaos

Staking is a core mechanic in the OrderNChaos ecosystem that allows $CHAOS token holders to earn rewards while strengthening the protocol. When users stake their $CHAOS tokens, they receive $prCHAOS tokens as rewards, creating a compounding mechanism that increases their $CHAOS holdings over time.

The Staking Process

How Staking Works

  1. Stake $CHAOS: Users lock their $CHAOS tokens in the staking contract.
  2. Earn $prCHAOS: Stakers receive $prCHAOS rewards based on their proportion of the total staked $CHAOS.
  3. Claim and Convert: Users can claim their $prCHAOS rewards and convert them to $CHAOS.
  4. Compound (Optional): Users can stake their newly acquired $CHAOS to increase their stake and future rewards.

Why Stake?

Staking provides multiple benefits:

  • Earn $prCHAOS rewards that can be converted to more $CHAOS
  • Qualify for Zero Risk Loans by using staked $CHAOS as collateral
  • Contribute to protocol stability by reducing circulating supply
  • Potential governance rights in future protocol upgrades

$prCHAOS Reward Mechanism

Reward Distribution

$prCHAOS rewards are distributed to stakers based on the following principles:

  • Proportional Distribution: Rewards are distributed proportionally to each user's share of the total staked $CHAOS.
  • Continuous Accrual: Rewards accrue continuously while $CHAOS is staked.
  • Non-Transferable: $prCHAOS tokens cannot be transferred or traded, only redeemed for $CHAOS.
  • Dynamic Emission: The emission rate of $prCHAOS is calibrated to provide sustainable rewards.

Reward Calculation

The amount of $prCHAOS rewards a staker earns is calculated using the formula:

Rewards = (User_Staked_CHAOS / Total_Staked_CHAOS) × Emission_Rate × Time_Period

Where:

  • User_Staked_CHAOS: The amount of $CHAOS the user has staked
  • Total_Staked_CHAOS: The total amount of $CHAOS staked in the protocol
  • Emission_Rate: The rate at which $prCHAOS is emitted per unit of time
  • Time_Period: The duration for which rewards are calculated

Example Calculation

Let's say you've staked 1,000 $CHAOS, and there are 100,000 $CHAOS tokens staked in total. If the emission rate is 0.1% per day, your daily rewards would be:

(1,000 / 100,000) × 0.1% × 100,000 = 1 $prCHAOS per day

Over a month (30 days), you would earn approximately 30 $prCHAOS tokens, which could be redeemed for additional $CHAOS tokens.

Converting $prCHAOS to $CHAOS

The Conversion Process

When users are ready to convert their $prCHAOS rewards to $CHAOS, they follow these steps:

  1. Claim Rewards: Users claim their accumulated $prCHAOS rewards from the staking contract.
  2. Convert to $CHAOS: Users convert their $prCHAOS to $CHAOS at the current conversion rate.
  3. Receive $CHAOS: The protocol mints new $CHAOS tokens to the user's wallet, and the $prCHAOS tokens are burned.

Conversion Rate

The conversion rate from $prCHAOS to $CHAOS is designed to create sustainable value for stakers. It may adjust based on various protocol parameters to ensure long-term economic stability.

Currently, the conversion rate is set such that each $prCHAOS token can be redeemed for a fraction of a $CHAOS token, with the exact rate determined by the protocol's algorithm.

Compounding Strategy

Maximizing Returns Through Compounding

To maximize staking returns, users can implement a compounding strategy:

  1. Regular Claiming: Claim $prCHAOS rewards at optimal intervals (balancing gas costs with compounding benefits).
  2. Convert and Restake: Convert $prCHAOS to $CHAOS and immediately restake the newly acquired $CHAOS.
  3. Repeat: Continue this process to benefit from compounding returns.

Compounding Example

Let's compare simple staking versus compounding over a year:

Scenario: Starting with 1,000 $CHAOS with a 20% annual reward rate

  • Simple Staking (claiming once at year-end):
    End of Year: 1,000 $CHAOS + 200 $CHAOS (from converted $prCHAOS) = 1,200 $CHAOS
    Total Return: 20%
  • Monthly Compounding:
    End of Year: ~1,219 $CHAOS
    Total Return: ~21.9%

The power of compounding increases your returns by almost 2% in this example.

Staking While Using Zero Risk Loans

One of the unique features of OrderNChaos is the ability to continue earning staking rewards even while using staked $CHAOS as collateral for Zero Risk Loans:

  1. Stake $CHAOS: Stake your $CHAOS tokens in the protocol.
  2. Mint $ORDER: Use your staked $CHAOS as collateral to mint $ORDER stablecoins.
  3. Continue Earning: Your staked $CHAOS continues to earn $prCHAOS rewards even while serving as collateral.
  4. Compound Normally: Claim and convert your $prCHAOS rewards as usual to increase your staked position.

This mechanism creates a form of "negative interest" loan, where borrowers earn yield on their collateral instead of paying interest.

Staking Risks and Considerations

Key Considerations

Consideration Details
Lockup Period There is no mandatory lockup period. Users can unstake their $CHAOS at any time (unless it's being used as collateral for an active Zero Risk Loan).
Gas Costs Consider the gas costs when claiming and converting rewards. For smaller positions, it may be more efficient to claim less frequently.
Emission Changes The emission rate of $prCHAOS may change over time based on protocol parameters, potentially affecting your returns.
Redemption Rate The rate at which $prCHAOS can be converted to $CHAOS may vary, impacting the value of your rewards.

Getting Started with Staking

To begin staking your $CHAOS tokens and earning $prCHAOS rewards, follow these steps:

  1. Acquire $CHAOS: Purchase $CHAOS tokens through the protocol's instant-buy market.
  2. Access the Staking Interface: Navigate to the "Stake" section of the OrderNChaos app.
  3. Choose Staking Amount: Select the amount of $CHAOS you wish to stake.
  4. Confirm Transaction: Approve the staking transaction through your wallet.
  5. Monitor Rewards: Track your accumulating $prCHAOS rewards in the app dashboard.
  6. Claim and Compound: Periodically claim your rewards, convert them to $CHAOS, and restake for optimal returns.

For more detailed information about the $prCHAOS token itself, see the $prCHAOS Token section.
