Introduction to $prCHAOS

$prCHAOS (pre-CHAOS) is a reward token in the OrderNChaos ecosystem that incentivizes users to stake their $CHAOS tokens. Unlike many reward tokens in DeFi, $prCHAOS is designed with a specific redemption mechanism that creates sustainable APY for stakers.

What is $prCHAOS?

$prCHAOS is a non-transferable token that is distributed to $CHAOS stakers as a reward. It represents a claim on newly minted $CHAOS tokens, allowing stakers to increase their $CHAOS holdings over time.

Key Features of $prCHAOS

  • Staking Rewards: $prCHAOS is earned by staking $CHAOS tokens in the protocol.
  • Non-Transferable: $prCHAOS cannot be transferred or traded; it can only be redeemed for $CHAOS.
  • Redemption Mechanism: $prCHAOS can be redeemed for $CHAOS at a rate determined by the protocol.
  • Emission Schedule: New $prCHAOS tokens are minted at regular intervals based on the total supply of $CHAOS.

How $prCHAOS Works

Earning $prCHAOS

When users stake their $CHAOS tokens in the protocol, they begin earning $prCHAOS rewards. These rewards are distributed proportionally based on each user's share of the total staked $CHAOS.

Example of Earning $prCHAOS

If there are 1,000,000 $CHAOS tokens staked in total, and you have staked 10,000 $CHAOS (1% of the total), you would receive 1% of the $prCHAOS emissions for each reward period.

$prCHAOS Emission

The protocol mints new $prCHAOS tokens at regular intervals. The amount of $prCHAOS minted in each period is calculated based on the total supply of $CHAOS, using the following formula:

Redeeming $prCHAOS for $CHAOS

When users have accumulated $prCHAOS, they can redeem it for $CHAOS tokens. The redemption process works as follows:

  1. Users claim their $prCHAOS rewards from the staking contract.
  2. They then redeem the $prCHAOS for $CHAOS at the current redemption rate.
  3. The $prCHAOS tokens are burned, and the user receives newly minted $CHAOS tokens.

The redemption rate of $prCHAOS to $CHAOS is determined by the protocol and may be subject to a dynamic mechanism to ensure balanced growth.

The Compounding Effect

One of the key benefits of the $prCHAOS system is the ability to create a compounding effect:

  1. Stake $CHAOS to earn $prCHAOS rewards.
  2. Redeem $prCHAOS for new $CHAOS tokens.
  3. Stake the newly acquired $CHAOS to earn more $prCHAOS rewards.
  4. Repeat this process to compound your returns over time.

Compounding Example

If you stake 1,000 $CHAOS and earn 50 $prCHAOS over a period, you can redeem those 50 $prCHAOS for, say, 20 new $CHAOS tokens. By staking those 20 $CHAOS along with your original 1,000, you now have 1,020 $CHAOS staked, which will earn more $prCHAOS in the next period than your original 1,000 would have.

Getting Started with $prCHAOS

To start earning and using $prCHAOS, follow these steps:

  1. Acquire $CHAOS: Purchase $CHAOS tokens through the protocol's instant-buy market.
  2. Stake Your $CHAOS: Navigate to the "Stake" section of the OrderNChaos app and stake your $CHAOS.
  3. Earn $prCHAOS: Your staked $CHAOS will automatically begin earning $prCHAOS rewards.
  4. Claim and Redeem: Periodically claim your $prCHAOS rewards and redeem them for $CHAOS.
  5. Compound: Stake your newly acquired $CHAOS to create a compounding effect.

For more detailed information about staking and rewards, see the Staking Rewards section.
