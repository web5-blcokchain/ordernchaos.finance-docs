Introduction to $CHAOS

$CHAOS is the algorithmic metastable token at the heart of the OrderNChaos ecosystem. Unlike traditional cryptocurrencies, $CHAOS combines the growth potential of volatile assets with the security features of stable assets through its unique floor price mechanism.

Core Properties of $CHAOS

  • Algorithmic Metastable Token: $CHAOS is designed to maintain stability while also capturing upside potential.
  • Hard Floor Price: $CHAOS has a guaranteed minimum value (floor price) backed by the protocol's reserve assets.
  • Rising Floor Mechanism: As surplus value accrues to the protocol, the floor price of $CHAOS increases over time, locking in gains permanently.
  • Collateral for $ORDER: $CHAOS can be used as collateral to mint $ORDER stablecoins with zero liquidation risk.
  • Stakeable for Rewards: $CHAOS can be staked to earn $prCHAOS rewards, which compound over time.

The $CHAOS Value Model

The value of $CHAOS is determined by several key factors:

1. Intrinsic Value (Floor Price)

The floor price of $CHAOS is derived from the protocol's reserve assets, which include a diverse basket of stablecoins and other assets. This floor price represents the minimum value that $CHAOS can have, as the protocol guarantees to buy back $CHAOS at this price.

2. Market Price

The market price of $CHAOS is determined by supply and demand in the protocol's virtual Automated Market Maker (vAMM). The market price can exceed the floor price, but it cannot fall below it due to the protocol's buyback guarantee.

3. Rising Floor Mechanism

As the protocol accumulates surplus value (from fees, market operations, etc.), a portion of this surplus is used to increase the floor price of $CHAOS. This mechanism ensures that $CHAOS holders benefit from the protocol's growth and that these gains are permanently locked in.

Example of Rising Floor

If the current floor price of $CHAOS is $10, and the protocol accumulates surplus value, it might increase the floor price to $10.50. This means that $CHAOS can never be worth less than $10.50, effectively locking in a 5% gain for all $CHAOS holders.

$CHAOS Utility in the Ecosystem

Staking for $prCHAOS Rewards

When $CHAOS is staked in the protocol, it earns $prCHAOS rewards which can be claimed and converted back to $CHAOS. This creates a compounding effect that increases the holder's $CHAOS position over time. For details, see the Staking Rewards section.

Collateral for $ORDER Loans

Staked $CHAOS can be used as collateral to mint $ORDER tokens. Since these loans are priced at the floor value of $CHAOS (which can never be breached), there is zero liquidation risk. This allows for leverage without the typical risks associated with collateralized debt positions. For details, see the Zero Risk Loans section.

Governance Potential

In future versions of the protocol, $CHAOS holders may be given governance rights to vote on protocol parameters and upgrades.

Getting Started with $CHAOS

To start using $CHAOS, follow these steps:

  1. Acquire $CHAOS: Purchase $CHAOS tokens through the protocol's instant-buy market using stablecoins.
  2. Stake Your $CHAOS: Navigate to the "Stake" section of the OrderNChaos app and stake your $CHAOS to start earning $prCHAOS rewards.
  3. Compound Your Position: Periodically claim your $prCHAOS rewards and convert them back to $CHAOS, then stake the newly acquired $CHAOS to create a compounding effect.
  4. Consider Leverage (Advanced): For advanced users, consider minting $ORDER against your staked $CHAOS and using that $ORDER to purchase more $CHAOS, creating leveraged exposure with zero liquidation risk.

For more information about the relationship between $CHAOS and $prCHAOS, see the $prCHAOS page.
