Fee Structure Overview

The OrderNChaos protocol implements a carefully designed fee structure that balances user experience with protocol sustainability. All fees collected are used to benefit the ecosystem in various ways, including increasing the floor price of $CHAOS, funding development, and ensuring long-term protocol stability.

Fee Philosophy

OrderNChaos follows a "value-for-value" approach to fees. We charge fees only where value is being created or exchanged, and we ensure that the fees collected are reinvested into the protocol to create even more value for all participants.

vAMM Trading Fees

Fees charged on trades made through the virtual Automated Market Maker.

Action Fee Rate Fee Distribution
Buy $CHAOS 0.5% 80% to floor price, 20% to protocol treasury
Sell $CHAOS 0.5% 80% to floor price, 20% to protocol treasury

Example: If you buy $1,000 worth of $CHAOS, you will pay a fee of $5 (0.5% of $1,000). Of this fee, $4 (80%) will contribute to increasing the floor price of $CHAOS, and $1 (20%) will go to the protocol treasury.

Staking and Reward Fees

Fees related to staking $CHAOS and claiming $prCHAOS rewards.

Action Fee Rate Fee Distribution
Stake $CHAOS 0% N/A
Unstake $CHAOS 0% N/A
Claim $prCHAOS rewards 0% N/A
Convert $prCHAOS to $CHAOS 2% 100% to floor price

Example: If you convert 100 $prCHAOS to $CHAOS (equivalent to 40 $CHAOS at the current conversion rate), you will pay a fee of 0.8 $CHAOS (2% of 40 $CHAOS). This entire fee will contribute to increasing the floor price of $CHAOS.

Zero Risk Loan Fees

Fees associated with minting and redeeming $ORDER tokens against staked $CHAOS.

Action Fee Rate Fee Distribution
Mint $ORDER (borrow) 0.1% 50% to floor price, 50% to protocol treasury
Repay $ORDER loan 0% N/A

Example: If you mint 1,000 $ORDER against your staked $CHAOS, you will pay a one-time fee of 1 $ORDER (0.1% of 1,000 $ORDER). Of this fee, 0.5 $ORDER (50%) will contribute to increasing the floor price of $CHAOS, and 0.5 $ORDER (50%) will go to the protocol treasury.

Note on Negative Interest Rate

While there is a small one-time fee for minting $ORDER, borrowers continue to earn staking rewards on their collateralized $CHAOS, creating a net positive yield (negative interest rate) for borrowers. This is a unique feature of OrderNChaos's Zero Risk Loan system.

Stableswap Fees (Coming Soon)

Fees for exchanging $ORDER with other stablecoins through the Stableswap feature.

Action Fee Rate Fee Distribution
Swap $ORDER for stablecoins 0.04% 50% to stakers, 50% to protocol treasury
Swap stablecoins for $ORDER 0.04% 50% to stakers, 50% to protocol treasury

Example: If you swap 10,000 $ORDER for USDC, you will pay a fee of 4 $ORDER (0.04% of 10,000 $ORDER). Of this fee, 2 $ORDER (50%) will be distributed to $CHAOS stakers, and 2 $ORDER (50%) will go to the protocol treasury.

Gas Optimization

While the OrderNChaos protocol charges fees as outlined above, we've designed our smart contracts to be gas-efficient to minimize the additional transaction costs users pay to the blockchain network.

  • Batch Processing: Where possible, actions are combined to reduce the number of separate transactions required.
  • Gas Optimization: Smart contracts are optimized for minimal gas usage.
  • Layer 2 Deployment: The protocol is deployed on Layer 2 solutions like Arbitrum and Optimism to provide lower gas costs compared to Ethereum mainnet.

Fee Usage and Benefits

The fees collected by the OrderNChaos protocol serve several important purposes:

Floor Price Appreciation

A significant portion of fees is used to increase the floor price of $CHAOS. This benefits all $CHAOS holders by:

  • Increasing the minimum value of their $CHAOS tokens
  • Enhancing the stability and security of the protocol
  • Creating a permanent, irreversible increase in value (the floor price can only go up)

Protocol Treasury

Fees allocated to the treasury support the long-term development and sustainability of the protocol:

  • Funding ongoing development and new features
  • Supporting marketing and community growth initiatives
  • Providing a safety buffer for unexpected events
  • Eventually, supporting the transition to full DAO governance

Staker Rewards

Some fees (such as from the upcoming Stableswap feature) are distributed to $CHAOS stakers:

  • Creating additional yield opportunities for stakers
  • Incentivizing long-term participation in the protocol
  • Aligning the interests of users and stakers

Fee Adjustments

The fee structure described above represents the initial implementation. In the future, as the protocol transitions to community governance, fee rates and distribution may be adjusted through governance proposals to best serve the long-term interests of the protocol and its participants.
